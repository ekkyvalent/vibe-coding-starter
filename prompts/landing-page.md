# Starter Prompt: Landing Page

Copy and paste this into Claude Code to build a clean landing page.

---

## The prompt

```
Build me a clean marketing landing page. Here are the details:

PRODUCT NAME: [your product name]
TAGLINE: [one sentence that describes what it does]
WHO IT'S FOR: [describe your target user]
MAIN BENEFIT: [the #1 thing your product does for them]

The page should have these sections:
1. Hero section — big headline using the tagline, a subheadline expanding on the main benefit, and a CTA button that says [your CTA text, e.g. "Get Early Access"]
2. How it works — 3 simple steps explaining the product
3. Who it's for — a short paragraph or bullet list
4. Footer — just the product name and copyright year

Design: clean and minimal, white background, one accent color.

Use the existing Next.js app structure. Put everything in app/page.tsx. Use Tailwind CSS for styling.
```

---

## How to fill it in

Replace everything in `[square brackets]` with your own content before pasting. Example:

- `PRODUCT NAME: Notch`
- `TAGLINE: Track your milestones, not your tasks`
- `WHO IT'S FOR: Founders and solopreneurs who ship products`
- `MAIN BENEFIT: Stay focused on what matters without getting lost in task management`

---

## Refining it afterward

Once Claude builds the page, you can ask follow-up questions like:

- *"Make the hero section taller and more dramatic"*
- *"Change the accent color to indigo"*
- *"Add a section with three testimonial quotes"*
- *"Make the font larger throughout"*
- *"Add a FAQ section at the bottom"*
